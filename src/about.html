About us

From the remote wilderness of Afar to vibrant Addis Ababa, an Ethiopia vacation is what you make it. Discover Afar's volcanic wonders, satisfy your inner child in Bales's theme parks, be lost for words on the Aksum, or enjoy a Abay. We've got you covered with the best adventure packages in the Ethiopia, and a guide to the best times to visit the Land of the Free.
